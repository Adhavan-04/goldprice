Gold Price Prediction

This article is based on a study conducted to understand the relationship between gold price and selected factors influencing it, namely stock market, crude oil price, rupee dollar exchange rate, inflation and interest rate. Monthly price data for the period January 2000 to December 2018 was used for the study. The data was further split into two periods, period I from January 2000 to October 2011 during which the gold price exhibits a raising trend and period II from November 2011 to December 2018 where the gold price is showing a horizontal trend.
